The French Riviera

Everything you need to know about getting here and where to stay for our wedding celebration.

✈

Getting There

The French Riviera is easily accessible from major cities around the world.

🛫

By Air

Nice Côte d'Azur Airport (NCE) is the closest airport, located just 7km from Nice city center and 10km from Villefranche-sur-Mer.

Direct flights are available from most major European cities, as well as from New York, Dubai, and other international hubs.

🚌

By Train

Nice-Ville Station is well-connected via France's TGV high-speed rail network. Travel time from Paris is approximately 5.5 hours.

Book tickets at SNCF Connect.

🚗

Getting Around

We recommend renting a car to explore the area, though taxis and Uber are readily available. The local bus and tram systems are also excellent.

Transportation between wedding events will be provided.

🏠

Alternative Options

🏡

Vacation Rentals

For families or groups, consider renting an apartment or villa through Airbnb or VRBO. Many beautiful options are available in both Nice and Villefranche-sur-Mer.

🏖

Nearby Towns

Beaulieu-sur-Mer, Eze, and Cap-Ferrat are all within 15 minutes and offer additional accommodation options with beautiful scenery.

💡

Travel Tips

📅

Book Early

Late August/early September is peak season on the French Riviera. We recommend booking your accommodations as soon as possible to secure the best options and rates.

🌞

Weather

Expect warm Mediterranean weather with temperatures around 25-28°C (77-82°F). Evenings can be cooler, so bring a light layer for outdoor events.

💰

Currency

France uses the Euro (€). Credit cards are widely accepted, but it's good to have some cash for small purchases and tips.

📱

Connectivity

EU visitors can use their mobile data freely. Others should check with their provider about international plans or consider getting a local SIM card.
