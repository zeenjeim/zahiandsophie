The Côte d'Azur

Make the most of your time in one of the world's most beautiful destinations. Here are our favorite recommendations.

🌊

Beaches & Water

The French Riviera is famous for its stunning Mediterranean coastline and crystal-clear waters.

Villefranche-sur-Mer

Plage des Marinières

One of the most beautiful beaches on the Riviera. Soft sand, calm waters, and stunning views of the colorful waterfront. Perfect for swimming and relaxation.

📍 5 min walk from village
Nice

Promenade des Anglais

The iconic seafront promenade stretching 7km along the Bay of Angels. Perfect for morning walks, people-watching, and enjoying the Mediterranean sunset.

📍 Nice waterfront
Saint-Jean-Cap-Ferrat

Paloma Beach

A hidden gem with turquoise waters and a chic beach club. Beloved by locals and celebrities alike. Excellent for snorkeling.

📍 15 min from Villefranche
🏰

Charming Villages

Explore the picturesque hilltop villages and coastal towns that make this region so magical.

Must Visit

Èze Village

A stunning medieval village perched on a cliff 400m above the sea. Wander through narrow cobblestone streets, visit the exotic garden, and enjoy breathtaking panoramic views.

📍 15 min from Nice
Art Lovers

Saint-Paul-de-Vence

One of France's most visited villages, beloved by artists for centuries. Home to the Fondation Maeght and numerous galleries. Perfect for art enthusiasts.

📍 30 min from Nice
Historic

Monaco & Monte Carlo

The glamorous principality is just a short drive away. Visit the Prince's Palace, stroll through the Casino gardens, and explore the charming old town.

📍 20 min from Nice
Local Gem

Villefranche-sur-Mer Old Town

Don't miss exploring the village itself! The Citadel, Chapelle Saint-Pierre (decorated by Jean Cocteau), and the atmospheric Rue Obscure covered street.

📍 Wedding venue village
🌇

Exploring Nice

Our ceremony city has so much to offer. Here are some highlights not to miss.

Old Town

Vieux Nice

Lose yourself in the labyrinth of narrow streets filled with colorful buildings, bustling markets, and authentic Niçois restaurants. Visit Cours Saleya market for flowers and local produce.

Views

Castle Hill (Colline du Château)

Climb (or take the elevator) for the best views of Nice, the bay, and the port. Beautiful gardens, a waterfall, and the perfect spot for photos.

Culture

Musée Matisse

Dedicated to Henri Matisse who lived in Nice for nearly 40 years. A beautiful collection housed in a 17th-century Genoese villa surrounded by olive groves.

Culture

MAMAC

Museum of Modern and Contemporary Art with an impressive collection of New Realism, Pop Art, and Minimalism. Great rooftop terrace with city views.

🍴

Food & Drink

The French Riviera offers incredible culinary experiences. Here are some of our favorite spots.

Local Cuisine

Must-Try Dishes

Socca - Crispy chickpea pancake
Salade Niçoise - The original version
Pissaladière - Onion, anchovy & olive tart
Pan Bagnat - Niçoise sandwich

Villefranche

Waterfront Dining

The harbor is lined with restaurants serving fresh seafood with stunning views. Perfect for a long, relaxed lunch watching the boats go by.

Nice

Cours Saleya

The famous market square transforms into a lively restaurant scene in the evenings. Great for people-watching and sampling local specialties.

Wine

Provençal Rosé

When in the South of France, drink rosé! The local wines from Provence are world-famous. Visit a vineyard in Bellet, Nice's own wine appellation.

🚗

Day Trips

If you're extending your stay, these destinations are worth the journey.

1 Hour

Cannes

Famous for its film festival, beautiful beaches, and glamorous Boulevard de la Croisette. Worth a day trip for shopping and beach clubs.

1 Hour

Antibes & Cap d'Antibes

Charming old town with excellent Picasso museum, fantastic market, and beautiful coastal walks around the cape.

30 min

Menton

The last town before Italy, known for its lemons, pastel-colored buildings, and beautiful gardens. A quieter, authentic Riviera experience.
