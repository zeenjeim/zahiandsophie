Travel & Logistics

It depends on your nationality. EU/EEA citizens do not need a visa. US, UK, Canadian, and many other nationals can visit France for up to 90 days without a visa. Please check with your local French embassy or consulate for specific requirements based on your citizenship.

A rental car is not necessary for attending the wedding events, as transportation will be provided. However, if you'd like to explore the region before or after the wedding, having a car gives you the most flexibility. The coastal roads are beautiful and there's plenty of free parking in the area.

The French Riviera enjoys beautiful Mediterranean weather in late summer. Expect temperatures around 25-28°C (77-82°F) during the day, with slightly cooler evenings. Rain is rare but not impossible, so pack a light layer just in case. Don't forget sunscreen and sunglasses!
